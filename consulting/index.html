Process

Consulting Process

  1. 01

    Discovery

    Understand the business, its goals and current constraints.

  2. 02

    Research

    Gather evidence on the market, customers and competitive landscape.

  3. 03

    Analysis

    Interpret findings to surface patterns, risks and opportunities.

  4. 04

    Workshop

    Work through findings together to align on direction.

  5. 05

    Strategy

    Translate analysis into clear, prioritised direction.

  6. 06

    Implementation Plan

    Sequence the work required to move strategy into action.

  7. 07

    Follow-up

    Revisit progress and refine direction as the business evolves.
