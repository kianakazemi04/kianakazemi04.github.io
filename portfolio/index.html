Business Strategy

Overview

Overview

Business Strategy connects positioning, priorities and resource allocation into one coherent direction. It exists to answer a simple question: what should this business focus on, and why.

Founders and leadership teams typically turn to this work when growth has outpaced structure, or when too many competing priorities are pulling execution in different directions.

Challenges

Common Business Challenges

Lack of strategic direction

Weak positioning

Poor execution

Unclear priorities

Limited market validation

Scaling challenges

Go-To-Market uncertainty

Operational inefficiencies

Approach

My Strategic Process

  1. 01

    Discover

    Understand the business, the problem and the context before anything else.

  2. 02

    Research

    Gather evidence on the market, customers and competitive landscape.

  3. 03

    Analysis

    Interpret findings to identify patterns, risks and opportunities.

  4. 04

    Strategy

    Translate analysis into clear direction and prioritised decisions.

  5. 05

    Execution Roadmap

    Sequence the work required to move from strategy to action.

  6. 06

    Measurement

    Define how progress and outcomes will be tracked over time.

Founder Support

Overview

Overview

Founder Support is ongoing strategic partnership rather than a single deliverable. It exists to give founders a structured thinking partner for the decisions that don’t have obvious answers — from prioritisation to team design to how much to take on at once.

It typically benefits early-stage founders operating with limited time, limited resources and high uncertainty.

Fundraising

Overview

Overview

Fundraising readiness is the structured preparation that happens before a founder ever sits across from an investor. It exists to close the gap between a business’s potential and how clearly that potential can be understood, questioned and trusted by outside capital.

It typically benefits founders preparing for a raise who want their model, narrative and numbers to hold up under scrutiny.

Growth Strategy

Overview

Overview

Growth Strategy identifies where sustainable growth can realistically come from, and sequences the work required to get there. It exists because growth without structure tends to create complexity faster than it creates value.

It typically benefits businesses with early traction who need a deliberate plan for what to scale, and in what order.

Market Research & Strategic Analysis

Overview

Overview

Market Research & Strategic Analysis reduces uncertainty before decisions are made, not after. It exists to replace assumption with evidence — about customers, competitors and market dynamics.

It typically benefits founders and teams validating a new direction, market or business model before committing resources to it.
